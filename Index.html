    Ranger's Apprentice

    The story is based in the mythical world of Arluen, a medieval era with Kings and knights. The mysterious Ranger Corps, rumored to practice black magic, act as the King's eyes and ears throughout the country, and answer to no one but the King. However, the rest of the world has its own occupants and people. Travel the world through the 12 book series.
